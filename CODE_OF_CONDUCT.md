# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want CloakGuard to be a welcoming place to report bugs, suggest changes, improve documentation, and contribute code. People should be able to take part without harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful when opinions differ.
- Give useful, specific feedback.
- Accept corrections without turning them into personal arguments.
- Keep issue examples synthetic. Never post someone else's private information.
- Focus discussion on the project and the change being reviewed.

Harassment, personal attacks, sexualized language, threats, sustained disruption, or publishing private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard. Reasons will be explained when it is appropriate and safe to do so.

Report serious conduct concerns privately through the contact method listed on the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/benthompsondev). Do not put sensitive details in a public issue.
